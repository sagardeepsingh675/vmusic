Project Overview & Vision

vMusic aims to revolutionize the way users interact with their music. It's not just a player; it's a personal DJ that understands your taste, mood, and specific requests. The core vision is to create a seamless and intuitive music experience, moving beyond simple playback to intelligent, personalized curation and control.

Core Features

Intelligent Playlist Creation

Use natural language to request complex playlists.

Precise Playback Control

Start songs from specific timestamps or loop segments.

Personalized Curation

AI learns your habits to suggest music based on taste and time.

Voice-Activated Control

Use the wake word "vMusic" for hands-free control.

Unique Selling Propositions

Mood-Based Playlists

AI analyzes your voice and language to create the perfect vibe.

AI-Generated "Mashups"

Seamless transitions between songs for a continuous mix.

Social & Collaborative

Share playlists and collaborate with friends.

"Surprise Me" Feature

Get unexpected playlists based on a loose theme.
